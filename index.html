Announcement System

This is an announcement system. Everything was made by me.
The UI is customizable.
The time that the announcement lasts is calculated based on the average reading speed of a slow reader (13 characters/second.)
The time has also a minimum of 3 seconds and a maximum of 20.
Since Roblox ToS wants that every string inputted by players that is displayed to others must be filtered, the body of announcements gets filtered.
